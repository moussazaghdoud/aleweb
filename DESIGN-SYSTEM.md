# SECTION 1 â€” Modernized Global Information Architecture

## 1.1 Strategic IA Redesign Principles

The legacy IA treats "Products," "Solutions," and "Industries" as three parallel, disconnected silos. Users must already know which silo their need belongs to. The modernized IA introduces a **convergence layer** that cross-links all three dimensions while preserving every existing node.

### Three-Pillar Strategic Framework

The entire portfolio maps onto three strategic pillars that become the primary mental model:

| Pillar | Color | Scope | Products | Solutions |
|--------|-------|-------|----------|-----------|
| **Network** | Blue `#3B82F6` | Switching, WLAN, Industrial, Location Services | 46 products (switches, WLAN, asset tracking) | DAN, Autonomous Network, Data Center, SPB, etc. |
| **Cloud** | Purple `#9370DB` | UCaaS, CPaaS, Rainbow, Contact Center | 19 products (platforms, applications, integration) | DAC, Cloud Comms, UC, XaaS, Hybrid Workplace |

Every product and solution maps to at least one pillar. Cross-cutting items (Security, Business Continuity) span all three.
